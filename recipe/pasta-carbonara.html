Spaghetti Carbonara 


ALGORITHM Spaghetti Carbonara

INGREDIENTS
350 g spaghetti
150 g pancetta or guanciale, diced
3 large eggs
80 g Pecorino Romano, grated
80 g Parmesan, grated
Salt and black pepper

PROCESS
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Cook spaghetti UNTIL al dente.

Meanwhile:
Cook pancetta/guanciale in a pan UNTIL crispy.
Do NOT drain the fat — this is flavor.

In a bowl:
Whisk eggs + Pecorino + Parmesan.
Season generously with black pepper.

Drain pasta, reserving some cooking water.
Add pasta to pan with pancetta.
Remove pan from heat.

Quickly stir in egg-cheese mixture.
Add splash of pasta water UNTIL creamy.
Do NOT scramble the eggs.

Season with extra pepper.
Serve immediately with extra cheese.

OUTPUT
A rich, creamy carbonara made without cream —
just eggs, cheese, pasta water, and crispy pancetta.
