Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 350 g spaghetti
  • 150 g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 80 g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • 80 g Parmesan, grated
  • Salt and black pepper

Process

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti UNTIL al dente.
  2. Meanwhile: cook pancetta/guanciale in a pan UNTIL crispy. Do NOT drain the fat — this is flavor.
  3. In a bowl: whisk eggs + Pecorino + Parmesan. Season generously with black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some cooking water. Add pasta to pan with pancetta. Remove pan from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in egg-cheese mixture. Add splash of pasta water UNTIL creamy. Do NOT scramble the eggs.
  6. Season with extra pepper. Serve immediately with extra cheese.

Result

A rich, creamy carbonara made without cream — just eggs, cheese, pasta water, and crispy pancetta.
