Roasted Sweet Potato & Chickpea Salad 


ALGORITHM Mediterranean Sweet Potato Salad

------------------------------------------------------------
ROASTED VEGETABLES

INGREDIENTS
2 sweet potatoes, diced
1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 onion, sliced
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp ground cumin
Salt
Black pepper

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 200°C.

Place sweet potatoes, chickpeas, and onion on a baking tray.
Drizzle with olive oil.
Season with paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper.

Mix well until evenly coated.
Roast for 30–40 minutes,
stirring halfway through,
UNTIL the sweet potatoes are tender and lightly caramelized.

------------------------------------------------------------
SALAD

INGREDIENTS
Iceberg lettuce
1 bell pepper, diced
2 tomatoes, diced
150 g feta cheese, crumbled

PROCESS
Wash and chop the lettuce.
Dice the bell pepper and tomatoes.
Place everything in a large serving bowl.

------------------------------------------------------------
YOGHURT DRESSING

INGREDIENTS
200 g plain yoghurt
1 garlic clove, minced or use garlic powder
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp honey (optional) 
Salt
Black pepper

PROCESS
Mix all ingredients together
UNTIL smooth and creamy.

------------------------------------------------------------
ASSEMBLY

PROCESS
Add the warm roasted vegetables
to the salad.

Sprinkle with crumbled feta.

Drizzle with the yoghurt dressing
or serve it on the side.

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT
A colourful, healthy salad with roasted sweet potatoes,
crispy chickpeas, fresh vegetables,
creamy feta, and a refreshing yoghurt dressing.
Perfect as a light dinner or lunch.
