Gevulde Speculaas (Dutch Spiced Almond Pastry)

Ingredients

  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 150 g light brown sugar
  • 125 g unsalted butter
  • 10 g speculaas spice mix
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 25 ml milk
  • 125 g almonds
  • 125 g granulated sugar
  • Zest of ½ organic lemon
  • 1 egg white
  • 20–25 whole almonds
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp milk or heavy cream

OPTIONAL — Homemade speculaas spice mix

  • 8 tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp ground cloves
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 2 tsp mace
  • 2 tsp cardamom

Process

  1. Combine flour + sugar + butter + spice mix + baking powder + salt + milk. Mix UNTIL cohesive dough forms. Refrigerate ≥ 30 minutes.
  2. Pulse almonds UNTIL finely ground. Add sugar + lemon zest + egg white. Pulse UNTIL almond paste forms.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Prepare baking dish.
  4. Divide dough INTO 2 equal portions. Roll out first portion and place as base layer.
  5. Spread almond paste evenly.
  6. Roll out second portion. Place over filling. Seal edges.
  7. Whisk egg yolk + milk. Brush top layer. Arrange whole almonds. Brush again.
  8. Bake 40 minutes at 180°C. Cool completely before slicing.

Result

Golden-brown spiced pastry with soft almond center. Best sliced when fully cooled.
