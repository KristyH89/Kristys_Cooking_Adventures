Oven-Baked Wild Boar Ribs with Braised Red Cabbage & Mashed Potatoes

Wild Boar Ribs

Ingredients

  • 1 rack wild boar ribs (or pork ribs)

Dry Rub

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp coarse sea salt
  • 2 tsp mild paprika powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder

Glaze

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 75 ml soy sauce
  • 100 ml ketjap manis
  • 5 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Process (Ribs)

Process

  1. Mix dry rub ingredients. Remove membrane from ribs. Rub ribs generously.
  2. Marinate ≥ 1 hour (preferably overnight).
  3. Preheat oven to 110–115°C. Bake ribs 3 hours.
  4. Wrap ribs tightly with splash of apple juice/cola/water. Bake 2 more hours.
  5. Prepare glaze: sauté garlic + onion in olive oil. Add soy sauce + ketjap + honey.
  6. Unwrap ribs. Brush with glaze. Bake 1 hour, brushing several times UNTIL sticky and caramelized.

Braised Red Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 small red cabbage (~800 g), sliced
  • 50 g butter
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 30 g sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 100 ml apple juice
  • 2 apples, diced
  • Salt + pepper

Process

  1. Melt butter. Cook onion UNTIL soft.
  2. Add cabbage + vinegar + sugar + spices.
  3. Add apple juice. Simmer 45–60 minutes.
  4. Add apples last 10–15 minutes.
  5. Remove spices before serving.

Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1.5–2 kg potatoes
  • Butter
  • Warm milk (or cooking water)
  • Salt

Process

  1. Cook potatoes UNTIL tender. Drain.
  2. Add butter + warm milk. Mash UNTIL smooth.
  3. Season with salt.

Serving Suggestion

Process

  1. Serve ribs with braised red cabbage and mashed potatoes.
  2. Brush extra glaze over ribs.

Result

Tender glazed wild boar ribs with sweet braised red cabbage and creamy mashed potatoes.
