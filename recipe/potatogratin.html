Easy Oven-Baked Potato Gratin

Ingredients

  • ~800 g ready-made potato gratin (serves 2)
  • 150 g bacon pieces (or any preferred substitute)
  • Mixed frozen vegetables (or fresh, any kind you like, no need to cut frozen ones)
  • Grated cheese
  • Oven-safe baking dish

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Add mixed frozen vegetables to the baking dish.
  3. Spread the bacon pieces evenly on top.
  4. Add the potato gratin over the bacon and vegetables.
  5. Finish with a generous layer of grated cheese.
  6. Bake for about 40 minutes, or until the top is golden and the gratin is bubbling.

Result

A simple, comforting oven dish that requires almost no effort — just layer everything in a dish and let the oven do the work.
