Kanelbullar (Swedish Cinnamon Buns) 


ALGORITHM Kanelbullar (Swedish Cinnamon Buns)

------------------------------------------------------------
DOUGH

INGREDIENTS
500 g flour
75 g sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cardamom
25 g fresh yeast (or 7 g dry yeast)
250 ml milk
75 g butter, softened

PROCESS
Warm milk gently (not hot).
Dissolve yeast in warm milk.

Add flour + sugar + salt + cardamom.
Mix UNTIL dough forms.

Add softened butter.
Knead 8–10 minutes UNTIL smooth and elastic.

Cover bowl.
Let rise 45–60 minutes UNTIL doubled.

------------------------------------------------------------
FILLING

INGREDIENTS
75 g butter, softened
50 g sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon

PROCESS
Mix butter + sugar + cinnamon INTO smooth paste.

------------------------------------------------------------
SHAPING

PROCESS
Roll dough INTO rectangle.
Spread cinnamon filling evenly.

Fold dough (letter fold).
Roll lightly.

Cut INTO strips.
Twist each strip INTO a knot.

Place on baking tray.
Cover and rise 30 minutes.

------------------------------------------------------------
BAKING

INGREDIENTS
1 egg (for brushing)
Pearl sugar (optional)

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 225°C.
Brush buns with beaten egg.
Sprinkle pearl sugar.

Bake 8–10 minutes UNTIL golden brown.

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT
Soft, fluffy Swedish cinnamon buns with buttery cinnamon filling
and a golden, glossy top — perfect with coffee.
