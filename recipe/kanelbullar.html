ALGORITHM Swedish Cinnamon Buns (Kanelbullar)

INGREDIENTS

DOUGH
500 g flour
75 g sugar
7 g dry yeast
300 ml milk (lukewarm)
75 g butter (melted)
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cardamom (optional but traditional)

FILLING
75 g butter (softened)
100 g brown sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon

TOPPING
1 egg (for brushing)
Pearl sugar (optional)

PROCESS

Warm the milk until lukewarm and mix in the yeast. Let it sit for a few minutes.

In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and cardamom.

Add the milk mixture and melted butter.

Knead the dough until smooth and elastic (about 8–10 minutes).

Cover and let the dough rise for 45–60 minutes, or until doubled in size.

Roll out the dough into a large rectangle.

Spread softened butter evenly over the dough.

Mix sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle it over the butter layer.

Roll the dough tightly into a log.

Cut into equal pieces and place them on a baking tray.

Cover and let rise for another 30–45 minutes.

Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with pearl sugar if using.

Bake at 200°C for 12–15 minutes, until golden brown.

OUTPUT

Soft and fluffy Swedish cinnamon buns with a rich cinnamon filling and a golden, slightly crisp top. Perfect with coffee or tea.
