Midsommar Tårta (Swedish Summer Strawberry Cake) 


ALGORITHM Midsommar Tårta (Swedish Summer Strawberry Cake)

------------------------------------------------------------
SPONGE CAKE

INGREDIENTS
3 eggs
150 g sugar
150 g flour
1 tsp baking powder
Pinch of salt

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 175°C.
Line a round cake pan.

Whisk eggs + sugar UNTIL pale and fluffy.
Mix flour + baking powder + salt.
Fold dry ingredients gently into egg mixture.

Pour batter into pan.
Bake 25–30 minutes UNTIL golden and springy.
Cool completely.

------------------------------------------------------------
FILLING

INGREDIENTS
300 ml heavy cream
1–2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
Fresh strawberries, sliced
OPTIONAL: strawberry jam

PROCESS
Whip cream + sugar + vanilla UNTIL soft peaks.
Slice strawberries.

Cut sponge cake INTO 2 layers.
Spread jam (optional).
Add whipped cream.
Add sliced strawberries.

Place top layer back on cake.

------------------------------------------------------------
TOPPING

INGREDIENTS
Fresh strawberries
Whipped cream (optional)
Powdered sugar

PROCESS
Decorate top with whole or halved strawberries.
Dust lightly with powdered sugar.
Add extra whipped cream if desired.

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT
A light, fluffy Swedish summer cake with fresh strawberries,
whipped cream, and soft vanilla sponge — perfect for Midsommar.
