Easy & Quick Noodles

Ingredients

  • Frozen meatballs (or any preferred protein: scrambled egg, fried egg, boiled egg)
  • 500 g mixed frozen vegetables (for 2 people)
  • Seasonings to taste: paprika powder, garlic powder, onion powder, ginger powder, curry powder
  • Sweet chili sauce
  • Kejap manis (or other liquid seasonings/sauces you like)
  • Noodles (amount depending on appetite)
  • Cooking oil

OPTIONAL

  • Top with sesame seeds for extra flavor and texture.

Process

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan.
  2. Add the frozen meatballs and mixed frozen vegetables.
  3. Cook until heated through and lightly browned.
  4. Season with your preferred spices: paprika, garlic, onion, ginger, curry, etc.
  5. Place the desired amount of noodles in a large bowl.
  6. Boil water and pour it over the noodles. Let them sit for a few minutes (depending on noodle type). Drain well.
  7. Add sweet chili sauce and kejap manis to the vegetables and meatballs. Mix well.
  8. Combine the noodles with the vegetable mixture. Stir thoroughly until everything is coated.

Result

A fast, flexible noodle dish packed with vegetables and flavor, perfect for busy days and easy to customize with whatever you have at home.
