Vanilla Quark Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 200 g (vanilla) quark
  • 50 g flour
  • 4 eggs
  • Butter or oil for frying

OPTIONAL TOPPING

  • 1–2 apples, sliced
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Butter for frying

Process

  1. Place quark, flour, and eggs in a bowl. Whisk UNTIL smooth and lump-free.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan. Pour small portions of batter to form mini pancakes. Cook 2–3 minutes per side UNTIL golden.
  3. For topping: melt butter in a pan. Add apples + cinnamon. Cook UNTIL soft and lightly caramelized.
  4. Serve pancakes warm with cinnamon apples.

Result

Light and fluffy vanilla quark pancakes with warm cinnamon apples.
