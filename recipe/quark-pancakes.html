ALGORITHM Vanilla quark Pancakes

INGREDIENTS

200 g (vanilla) quark

50 g flour

4 eggs

Butter or oil for frying

OPTIONAL TOPPING

1–2 apples, sliced

1 tsp cinnamon

A little butter for frying

PROCESS

Place the vanilla kvarg, flour, and eggs in a mixing bowl.

Whisk until the batter is smooth, light, and free of lumps.

Heat a little butter or oil in a frying pan over medium heat.

Pour small portions of batter into the pan to form mini pancakes.

Cook for 2–3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

Repeat until all batter has been used.

For the apple topping, melt a little butter in a separate pan.

Add the sliced apples and cinnamon.

Cook until the apples are soft and lightly caramelized.

Serve the pancakes warm with the cinnamon apples on top.

OUTPUT

Light and fluffy vanilla kvarg pancakes with a mild sweetness, perfectly paired with warm cinnamon apples.
