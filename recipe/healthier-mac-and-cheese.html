Healthier Mac and Cheese 


ALGORITHM Healthier Mac and Cheese

INPUT
250 g whole wheat pasta (or regular pasta)

30 g unsalted butter
30 g all-purpose flour
500 ml milk
150 g grated cheddar cheese
100 g herb cream cheese
Salt and black pepper to taste

1 red bell pepper, diced
2 carrots, grated or finely chopped
150 g mushrooms, sliced
100 g spinach, chopped or frozen spinach
1 zucchini, diced

OPTIONAL
Extra grated cheese for topping
Fresh parsley for garnish

PROCESS
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Cook pasta according to package instructions.
Drain and set aside.

Heat a large pan over medium heat.
Add bell pepper + carrots + mushrooms + zucchini.
Cook UNTIL vegetables become soft and lightly browned.

Add spinach.
Cook UNTIL wilted.
Set vegetables aside.

Melt butter in a saucepan.
Add flour.
Whisk continuously for 1–2 minutes.

Gradually add milk while whisking.
Cook UNTIL sauce thickens.

Add cheddar cheese + herb cream cheese.
Stir UNTIL smooth and creamy.

Season with salt and black pepper.

Combine pasta + vegetables + cheese sauce.
Mix UNTIL evenly coated.

OPTIONAL
Transfer mixture to a baking dish.
Sprinkle with extra grated cheese.
Bake 15–20 minutes at 200°C UNTIL golden on top.

Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

OUTPUT
Creamy macaroni and cheese packed with vegetables.
A healthier comfort-food dish with extra flavor, fiber, and nutrients.
