Healthier Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 250 g whole wheat pasta (or regular pasta)
  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • 30 g all-purpose flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • 150 g grated cheddar cheese
  • 100 g herb cream cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 carrots, grated or finely chopped
  • 150 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 100 g spinach, chopped or frozen spinach
  • 1 zucchini, diced

OPTIONAL

  • Extra grated cheese for topping
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Process

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add bell pepper + carrots + mushrooms + zucchini. Cook UNTIL vegetables become soft and lightly browned.
  3. Add spinach. Cook UNTIL wilted. Set vegetables aside.
  4. Melt butter in a saucepan. Add flour. Whisk continuously for 1–2 minutes.
  5. Gradually add milk while whisking. Cook UNTIL sauce thickens.
  6. Add cheddar cheese + herb cream cheese. Stir UNTIL smooth and creamy.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Combine pasta + vegetables + cheese sauce. Mix UNTIL evenly coated.
  9. OPTIONAL: Transfer mixture to a baking dish. Sprinkle with extra grated cheese. Bake 15–20 minutes at 200°C UNTIL golden on top.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Result

Creamy macaroni and cheese packed with vegetables. A healthier comfort-food dish with extra flavor, fiber, and nutrients.
