Quark Pizza Dough (High-Protein 2‑Ingredient Dough) 


ALGORITHM Quark Pizza Dough (High-Protein 2‑Ingredient Dough)

INGREDIENTS
250 g quark (kvarg)
250 g flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

PROCESS
Combine quark + flour + baking powder + salt.
Mix UNTIL dough forms.
Knead briefly UNTIL smooth.

Roll dough INTO pizza shape.
Add sauce + toppings of choice.

Bake at 200°C for 15–20 minutes
UNTIL crust is golden and toppings are cooked.

OPTIONAL MODULES
- Add garlic powder or Italian herbs to dough.
- Use whole‑wheat flour for extra fiber.
- Make mini pizzas or calzones.

OUTPUT
A soft, protein‑rich pizza dough made in minutes —
no yeast, no rising time, perfect for quick dinners.
