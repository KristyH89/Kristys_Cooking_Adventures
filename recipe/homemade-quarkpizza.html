ALGORITHM Quark Pizza Dough

INGREDIENTS

250 g flour

250 g quark (Greek-style yogurt or skyr can also be used)

1 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp salt

1 tbsp olive oil (optional, for extra softness)

PROCESS

Preheat the oven to 220°C.

Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.

Add the quark and mix until a dough forms.

If the dough is too sticky, add a little extra flour.

Knead for a few minutes until smooth.

Roll out the dough into one large pizza base or smaller individual pizzas.

Place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.

Add your favorite toppings (tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, meat, etc.).

Bake for 12–18 minutes, or until the crust is golden and crispy.

OUTPUT

A quick and easy homemade pizza with a soft yet crispy crust, made with quark for a lighter and protein-rich alternative to traditional dough.
