Quark Pizza Dough (High-Protein 2-Ingredient Dough)

Ingredients

  • 250 g quark (kvarg)
  • 250 g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

OPTIONAL MODULES

  • Add garlic powder or Italian herbs to dough.
  • Use whole-wheat flour for extra fiber.
  • Make mini pizzas or calzones.

Process

  1. Combine quark + flour + baking powder + salt. Mix UNTIL dough forms. Knead briefly UNTIL smooth.
  2. Roll dough INTO pizza shape. Add sauce + toppings of choice.
  3. Bake at 200°C for 15–20 minutes UNTIL crust is golden and toppings are cooked.

Result

A soft, protein-rich pizza dough made in minutes — no yeast, no rising time, perfect for quick dinners.
