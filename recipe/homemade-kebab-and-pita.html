Homemade Moose Kebab with Fresh Pita Bread and Garlic Sauce

Kebab

Ingredients


800 g moose mince (or beef mince, venison mince, or other minced meat)
1 finely grated onion
2 tsp paprika powder
1 tbsp paprika paste (or tomato paste + extra paprika powder)
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp Vegeta seasoning
Salt and black pepper to taste

Process


Preheat oven to 180°C.

Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.

Spread mixture thinly on baking paper.

Bake for 20–25 minutes until cooked and lightly browned.

Slice into kebab strips.

Pita Bread

Ingredients


500 g flour
300 ml lukewarm water
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp dried yeast
1 tsp salt

Process


Mix water and yeast, let sit briefly.

Knead flour, oil and yeast mixture into dough.

Add salt at the end and knead again.

Divide into 10 balls and flatten.

Let rise 30–40 minutes.

Bake at 230°C for ~10 minutes until puffed.

Garlic Yogurt Sauce

Ingredients


200 g Greek yogurt
2 tbsp mayonnaise
2 cloves garlic (grated or 1 tsp garlic powder)
1 tbsp parsley
Salt and pepper

Process


Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Let rest in fridge for 15 minutes before serving.

Serving Suggestion

Fill warm pita bread with kebab, add garlic sauce, and top with fresh vegetables.

OUTPUT

A complete homemade kebab meal featuring oven-baked moose kebab, freshly baked pita bread, creamy garlic yogurt sauce, and fresh vegetables.
