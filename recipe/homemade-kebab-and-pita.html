Homemade Kebab (Crispy Oven-Baked Kebab Strips) 


ALGORITHM Homemade Kebab (Crispy Oven-Baked Kebab Strips)

INGREDIENTS
500 g minced beef (10–12% fat)
1 tbsp paprika powder
1 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 175°C.

Place minced beef in a bowl.
Add all spices.
Mix UNTIL fully combined and smooth.

Shape mixture INTO a tight, compact loaf.
Place loaf on baking tray.

Bake 25 minutes at 175°C.
Remove from oven.
Let rest 10 minutes.

Slice loaf INTO very thin strips.
Increase oven temperature to 225°C.

Spread strips on baking tray.
Bake 5–10 minutes UNTIL crispy and browned.

OPTIONAL MODULES
- Add 1 tbsp yogurt for softer texture.
- Add 1 tbsp oil for richer flavor.
- Use lamb mince for döner-style kebab.

SERVING SUGGESTION
Serve in pita or flatbread with:
salad, tomatoes, cucumber, onion,
garlic sauce, kebab sauce, or chili sauce.

OUTPUT
Crispy, flavorful homemade kebab strips —
perfect for wraps, pita, bowls, or pizza.
