Wild Boar Steak with Carrot Mash, Glazed Carrots & Roasted Pumpkin 


ALGORITHM Wild Boar Steak with Carrot Mash, Glazed Carrots and Roasted Pumpkin

------------------------------------------------------------
CARROT MASH

INGREDIENTS
1.5 kg potatoes, peeled
500 g carrots, peeled and sliced
Butter to taste
Warm milk
Salt and black pepper

PROCESS
Peel and cut potatoes.
Slice carrots.
Cook together in salted water UNTIL tender.
Drain well.
Add butter + warm milk.
Mash UNTIL smooth and creamy.
Season with salt + pepper.

------------------------------------------------------------
WILD BOAR STEAK

INGREDIENTS
4 wild boar steaks
Butter or oil
Salt and black pepper
OPTIONAL: thyme or rosemary

PROCESS
Pat steaks dry.
Season generously.
Heat butter/oil in pan.
Cook steaks UNTIL browned on both sides.
Add herbs in final minutes.
Rest briefly before serving.

------------------------------------------------------------
GLAZED CARROTS

INGREDIENTS
3–4 carrots
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp honey
Pinch of salt

PROCESS
Peel carrots and cut into sticks.
Cook briefly UNTIL slightly tender.
Drain.
Melt butter in pan.
Add carrots + honey + salt.
Cook UNTIL glossy and caramelized.

------------------------------------------------------------
ROASTED PUMPKIN

INGREDIENTS
500 g pumpkin, cubed
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt and black pepper
50 g grated cheese
2 tbsp breadcrumbs
OPTIONAL: paprika or thyme

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 200°C.
Place pumpkin on tray.
Drizzle with oil.
Season with salt + pepper + spices.
Sprinkle cheese + breadcrumbs.
Roast 25–30 minutes UNTIL tender and golden.

------------------------------------------------------------
SERVING SUGGESTION
Serve carrot mash on warm plates.
Add wild boar steak.
Serve with glazed carrots + roasted pumpkin.
Finish with extra butter or meat juices.

OUTPUT
A comforting autumn meal with tender wild boar steak,
creamy carrot mash, sweet glazed carrots, and crispy roasted pumpkin.
