ALGORITHM Wild Boar Steak with Carrot Mash, Glazed Carrots and Roasted Pumpkin

CARROT MASH

INGREDIENTS

1.5 kg potatoes, peeled

500 g carrots, peeled and sliced

Butter to taste

Warm milk

Salt and black pepper to taste

PROCESS

Peel and cut the potatoes into evenly sized pieces.

Slice the carrots.

Cook the potatoes and carrots together in salted water until tender.

Drain well.

Add butter and warm milk.

Mash until smooth and creamy.

Season with salt and black pepper.


WILD BOAR STEAK

INGREDIENTS

4 wild boar steaks

Butter or oil for frying

Salt and black pepper

OPTIONAL

A few sprigs of fresh thyme or rosemary

PROCESS

Pat the steaks dry and season generously with salt and pepper.

Heat butter or oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.

Cook the steaks until nicely browned on both sides and cooked to your preferred doneness.

Add thyme or rosemary during the final minutes if desired.

Allow the meat to rest for a few minutes before serving.


GLAZED CARROTS

INGREDIENTS

3–4 carrots

1 tbsp butter

1 tbsp honey

Pinch of salt

PROCESS

Peel the carrots and cut into sticks or thick slices.

Cook briefly in boiling water until slightly tender.

Drain well.

Melt the butter in a frying pan.

Add the carrots, honey, and a pinch of salt.

Cook until glossy and lightly caramelized.


ROASTED PUMPKIN

INGREDIENTS

500 g pumpkin, cubed

1 tbsp olive oil

Salt and black pepper

50 g grated cheese

2 tbsp breadcrumbs

OPTIONAL

1 tsp paprika powder or dried thyme

PROCESS

Preheat the oven to 200°C.

Place the pumpkin cubes on a baking tray.

Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and optional spices.

Sprinkle the grated cheese and breadcrumbs evenly over the pumpkin.

Roast for 25–30 minutes until the pumpkin is tender and the topping is golden brown and crispy.

Serve immediately.


SERVING SUGGESTION

Spoon the carrot mash onto warm plates.

Place the wild boar steak on top or alongside.

Serve with glazed carrots and roasted pumpkin.

Finish with a little extra butter or meat juices if desired.


OUTPUT

A comforting autumn meal featuring tender wild boar steak, creamy carrot mash, sweet glazed carrots, and roasted pumpkin, bringing together rich game flavors and seasonal vegetables.
