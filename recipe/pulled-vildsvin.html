Pulled Wild Boar (or Pulled Pork)

Meat

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg wild boar shoulder or neck (or pork shoulder)

Dry Rub

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Liquid

Ingredients

  • 150 ml beef stock
  • 50 ml natural vinegar
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp ketjap manis

Process

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Cut meat into 3–4 large pieces. Mix dry rub ingredients and coat meat thoroughly.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a Dutch oven. Sear meat on all sides until browned.
  4. Mix stock + vinegar + soy sauce + ketjap. Pour into pot with meat.
  5. Cover tightly with lid or foil. Cook 3.5–4 hours.
  6. Check tenderness after 3.5 hours. If meat falls apart easily, remove from oven. If not, cook 30 minutes longer.
  7. Shred meat with two forks. Mix shredded meat with some cooking juices.

Result

Tender, slow-cooked pulled wild boar with smoky-sweet flavor and rich umami notes from soy sauce and ketjap manis.

Tip

Serve in homemade pita bread with fresh vegetables.
