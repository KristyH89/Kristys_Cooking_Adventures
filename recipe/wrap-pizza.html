ALGORITHM Easy Wrap Pizza

INGREDIENTS

1 large tortilla wrap

1 tbsp ketchup

1 tbsp tomato paste

1/2 tsp dried oregano

1/2 tsp dried basil

1/2 bell pepper, sliced

1/4 onion, thinly sliced (optional)

Salami slices (optional)

Grated cheese of choice

Fresh spring onion, sliced

Fresh basil leaves

PROCESS

Preheat the oven to 200°C.

Mix the ketchup and tomato paste in a small bowl.

Stir in the oregano and basil.

Place the tortilla wrap on a baking tray lined with baking paper.

Spread the sauce mixture evenly over the wrap.

Top with the bell pepper, onion (if using), salami, and grated cheese.

Bake for approximately 7 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the edges of the wrap are crispy.

Remove from the oven.

Finish with sliced spring onion and fresh basil leaves.

Serve immediately.

TIP

This recipe is highly customizable. Feel free to use any toppings you have available or enjoy eating. Popular options include ham, chicken, mushrooms, olives, corn, spinach, pineapple, different cheeses, or extra vegetables.

OUTPUT

A quick and crispy homemade wrap pizza with a simple tomato sauce and your favorite toppings, ready in just a few minutes and perfect for a fast lunch or easy weeknight meal.
