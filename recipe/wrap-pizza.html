Easy Wrap Pizza 


ALGORITHM Easy Wrap Pizza

INGREDIENTS
1 large tortilla wrap
1 tbsp ketchup
1 tbsp tomato paste
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 bell pepper, sliced
1/4 onion, thinly sliced (optional)
Salami slices (optional)
Grated cheese
Fresh spring onion
Fresh basil leaves

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 200°C.

Mix ketchup + tomato paste + oregano + basil.
Place wrap on baking tray.
Spread sauce evenly.

Top with bell pepper, onion, salami, cheese.
Bake ~7 minutes UNTIL cheese melts and edges are crispy.

Remove from oven.
Finish with spring onion + fresh basil.

TIP
Highly customizable — use any toppings you like:
ham, chicken, mushrooms, olives, corn, spinach, pineapple,
different cheeses, or extra vegetables.

OUTPUT
A quick, crispy homemade wrap pizza with simple tomato sauce
and your favorite toppings — perfect for a fast lunch or easy dinner.
