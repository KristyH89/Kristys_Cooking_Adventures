Patrch (Arabic Grilled Eggplant & Spiced Minced Meat Dish) 


ALGORITHM Patrch (Arabic Grilled Eggplant & Spiced Minced Meat Dish)

INGREDIENTS

EGGPLANT BASE
2 large aubergines (eggplants)
1–2 tbsp lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
2–3 tbsp thick Greek yogurt
1–2 tbsp tahini
Salt to taste

MEAT LAYER
400–500 g minced lamb or beef
1–2 tbsp butter or ghee
2–3 tbsp tomato paste
Salt and black pepper to taste
Spices (cumin, paprika, cinnamon, or Arabic spice blend)

TOPPING
Handful of almonds, toasted or fried
Fresh parsley, chopped

TO SERVE
Flatbread (tunnbröd or Arabic flatbread)
Fresh vegetables (tomato, paprika, cucumber)

PROCESS
Grill aubergines until skin is blackened and flesh soft.
Remove skin and cool completely.

Cook minced meat in butter or ghee.
Add tomato paste, salt, pepper, spices.
Cook until thick and rich.

Mash or chop aubergine.
Mix with lemon juice, garlic, tahini, yogurt, salt.

Spread aubergine mixture on serving plate.
Top with hot spiced meat.
Finish with almonds and parsley.

Serve with flatbread and fresh vegetables.

OUTPUT
A smoky, creamy Arabic-style dish with grilled aubergine, spiced meat,
tahini-yogurt cream, and crunchy almonds.
