Patrch (Arabic Grilled Eggplant & Spiced Minced Meat Dish)

Eggplant Base

Ingredients

  • 2 large aubergines (eggplants)
  • 1–2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2–3 tbsp thick Greek yogurt
  • 1–2 tbsp tahini
  • Salt to taste

Meat Layer

Ingredients

  • 400–500 g minced lamb or beef
  • 1–2 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 2–3 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, cinnamon, or Arabic spice blend)

Topping

Ingredients

  • Handful of almonds, toasted or fried
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

To Serve

Ingredients

  • Flatbread (tunnbröd or Arabic flatbread)
  • Fresh vegetables (tomato, paprika, cucumber)

Process

Process

  1. Grill aubergines until skin is blackened and flesh soft. Remove skin and cool completely.
  2. Cook minced meat in butter or ghee. Add tomato paste, salt, pepper, spices. Cook until thick and rich.
  3. Mash or chop aubergine. Mix with lemon juice, garlic, tahini, yogurt, salt.
  4. Spread aubergine mixture on serving plate. Top with hot spiced meat. Finish with almonds and parsley.
  5. Serve with flatbread and fresh vegetables.

Result

A smoky, creamy Arabic-style dish with grilled aubergine, spiced meat, tahini-yogurt cream, and crunchy almonds.
