ALGORITHM Patrch (Arabic Grilled Eggplant & Spiced Minced Meat Dish)

INGREDIENTS

EGGPLANT BASE
2 large aubergines (eggplants)

1–2 tbsp lemon juice

2 cloves garlic, minced

2–3 tbsp thick Greek yogurt

1–2 tbsp tahini

Salt to taste

MEAT LAYER
400–500 g minced lamb or beef

1–2 tbsp butter or ghee

2–3 tbsp tomato paste

Salt and black pepper to taste

Spices (e.g. cumin, paprika, cinnamon, or mixed Arabic spice blend)

TOPPING
Handful of almonds, toasted or fried

Fresh parsley, chopped

TO SERVE
Flatbread (e.g. tunnbröd or Arabic flatbread)

Fresh raw vegetables (tomato, paprika, cucumber)

PROCESS

A day in advance, grill the aubergines over an open flame or in the oven until the skin is fully blackened and the flesh is soft and smoky.

Remove the skin and let the aubergine flesh cool completely.

Cook the minced meat in butter or ghee over medium heat.

Add tomato paste, salt, pepper, and spices. Stir and cook until thick, rich, and well combined.

Chop or mash the grilled aubergine and place it in a bowl.

Mix in lemon juice, garlic, tahini, Greek yogurt, and salt until creamy but still slightly textured.

Spread the aubergine mixture evenly on a serving plate.

Top with the hot spiced minced meat.

Finish with toasted almonds and fresh parsley.

Serve with flatbread and fresh raw vegetables on the side.

OUTPUT

A smoky and creamy Arabic-style dish featuring grilled aubergine, spiced minced meat, tahini-yogurt cream, and crunchy almonds, traditionally served with flatbread and fresh vegetables.
