Tiramisu 


ALGORITHM Tiramisu

INPUT
1 container mascarpone
1 container heavy cream
±120 g sugar
1–2 tsp almond extract
1 package ladyfingers
1–2 cups brewed coffee
Cocoa powder (for dusting)

PROCESS
1. Brew coffee.
2. Transfer coffee to dipping bowl.
3. Add almond extract.

4. Whip mascarpone + heavy cream + sugar
   UNTIL stiff and creamy.

5. Select deep serving dish.

6. Spread mascarpone mixture (base layer).

7. FOR each layer of ladyfingers:
      Dip ladyfingers in coffee (2–3 seconds).
      Place over mascarpone layer.
      Add new mascarpone layer.
   END FOR

8. Finish with mascarpone layer on top.

9. Cover dish.
10. Refrigerate ≥ 4 hours.

11. Before serving:
      Dust with cocoa powder.

OPTIONAL MODULES
- Replace almond extract WITH amaretto.
- Reduce sugar to taste.
- Add strawberry jam layer.
- Top with fresh strawberries.

OUTPUT
Chilled, layered tiramisu ready to serve.
