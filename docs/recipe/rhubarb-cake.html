Greek Yogurt Cake with Rhubarb (or Apple Variation) 


ALGORITHM Greek Yogurt Cake with Rhubarb (or Apple Variation)

INGREDIENTS
250 g Greek yogurt
75 g olive oil
2 eggs
Zest of 1 lemon
1 tsp vanilla extract
250 g white sugar
225 g flour
2 tsp baking powder
Pinch of salt
300 g rhubarb (or apples)
1 tbsp cane sugar

OPTIONAL APPLE VARIATION
1–2 apples, peeled and sliced

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 175°C.
Line or grease a loaf pan.

Mix yogurt + olive oil + eggs + lemon zest + vanilla + sugar UNTIL smooth.

In separate bowl:
Combine flour + baking powder + salt.

Add dry ingredients to wet mixture.
Stir UNTIL fully combined.

Prepare rhubarb/apples.
Layer batter and fruit in 3 layers, ending with fruit on top.

Sprinkle cane sugar on top.

Bake 55–60 minutes UNTIL skewer comes out clean.

Cool in pan briefly.
Transfer to rack to cool completely.

Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

OUTPUT
A moist Greek yogurt cake with tangy rhubarb or sweet apple,
lightly caramelized on top and perfect with coffee or tea.

TIP
Swap fruit seasonally: rhubarb in spring, berries in summer,
apple/pear with cinnamon in autumn.
