Vanilla Quark Pancakes 


ALGORITHM Vanilla Quark Pancakes

INGREDIENTS
200 g (vanilla) quark
50 g flour
4 eggs
Butter or oil for frying

OPTIONAL TOPPING
1–2 apples, sliced
1 tsp cinnamon
Butter for frying

PROCESS
Place quark, flour, and eggs in a bowl.
Whisk UNTIL smooth and lump-free.

Heat butter or oil in a pan.
Pour small portions of batter to form mini pancakes.
Cook 2–3 minutes per side UNTIL golden.

For topping:
Melt butter in a pan.
Add apples + cinnamon.
Cook UNTIL soft and lightly caramelized.

Serve pancakes warm with cinnamon apples.

OUTPUT
Light and fluffy vanilla quark pancakes with warm cinnamon apples.
