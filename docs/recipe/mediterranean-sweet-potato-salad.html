Roasted Sweet Potato & Chickpea Salad

Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes, diced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place sweet potatoes, chickpeas, and onion on a baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil. Season with paprika, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Mix well until evenly coated. Roast for 30–40 minutes, stirring halfway through, UNTIL the sweet potatoes are tender and lightly caramelized.

Salad

Ingredients

  • Iceberg lettuce
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 150 g feta cheese, crumbled

Process

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce. Dice the bell pepper and tomatoes.
  2. Place everything in a large serving bowl.

Yoghurt Dressing

Ingredients

  • 200 g plain yoghurt
  • 1 garlic clove, minced or use garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Process

  1. Mix all ingredients together UNTIL smooth and creamy.

Assembly

Process

  1. Add the warm roasted vegetables to the salad.
  2. Sprinkle with crumbled feta.
  3. Drizzle with the yoghurt dressing or serve it on the side.

Result

A colourful, healthy salad with roasted sweet potatoes, crispy chickpeas, fresh vegetables, creamy feta, and a refreshing yoghurt dressing. Perfect as a light dinner or lunch.
