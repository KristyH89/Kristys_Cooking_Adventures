Kanelbullar (Swedish Cinnamon Buns)

Dough

Ingredients

  • 500 g flour
  • 75 g sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 25 g fresh yeast (or 7 g dry yeast)
  • 250 ml milk
  • 75 g butter, softened

Process

  1. Warm milk gently (not hot). Dissolve yeast in warm milk.
  2. Add flour + sugar + salt + cardamom. Mix UNTIL dough forms.
  3. Add softened butter. Knead 8–10 minutes UNTIL smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover bowl. Let rise 45–60 minutes UNTIL doubled.

Filling

Ingredients

  • 75 g butter, softened
  • 50 g sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon

Process

  1. Mix butter + sugar + cinnamon INTO smooth paste.

Shaping

Process

  1. Roll dough INTO rectangle. Spread cinnamon filling evenly.
  2. Fold dough (letter fold). Roll lightly.
  3. Cut INTO strips. Twist each strip INTO a knot.
  4. Place on baking tray. Cover and rise 30 minutes.

Baking

Ingredients

  • 1 egg (for brushing)
  • Pearl sugar (optional)

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 225°C.
  2. Brush buns with beaten egg. Sprinkle pearl sugar.
  3. Bake 8–10 minutes UNTIL golden brown.

Result

Soft, fluffy Swedish cinnamon buns with buttery cinnamon filling and a golden, glossy top — perfect with coffee.
