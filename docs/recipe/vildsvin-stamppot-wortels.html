Wild Boar Steak with Carrot Mash, Glazed Carrots & Roasted Pumpkin

Carrot Mash

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg potatoes, peeled
  • 500 g carrots, peeled and sliced
  • Butter to taste
  • Warm milk
  • Salt and black pepper

Process

  1. Peel and cut potatoes. Slice carrots.
  2. Cook together in salted water UNTIL tender. Drain well.
  3. Add butter + warm milk. Mash UNTIL smooth and creamy.
  4. Season with salt + pepper.

Wild Boar Steak

Ingredients

  • 4 wild boar steaks
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Thyme or rosemary (optional)

Process

  1. Pat steaks dry. Season generously.
  2. Heat butter/oil in pan. Cook steaks UNTIL browned on both sides.
  3. Add herbs in final minutes.
  4. Rest briefly before serving.

Glazed Carrots

Ingredients

  • 3–4 carrots
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of salt

Process

  1. Peel carrots and cut into sticks.
  2. Cook briefly UNTIL slightly tender. Drain.
  3. Melt butter in pan. Add carrots + honey + salt.
  4. Cook UNTIL glossy and caramelized.

Roasted Pumpkin

Ingredients

  • 500 g pumpkin, cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 50 g grated cheese
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • Paprika or thyme (optional)

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place pumpkin on tray. Drizzle with oil. Season with salt + pepper + spices.
  3. Sprinkle cheese + breadcrumbs.
  4. Roast 25–30 minutes UNTIL tender and golden.

Serving Suggestion

Process

  1. Serve carrot mash on warm plates.
  2. Add wild boar steak.
  3. Serve with glazed carrots + roasted pumpkin.
  4. Finish with extra butter or meat juices.

Result

A comforting autumn meal with tender wild boar steak, creamy carrot mash, sweet glazed carrots, and crispy roasted pumpkin.
