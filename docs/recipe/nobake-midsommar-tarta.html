No-Bake Midsommar Tårta

Ingredients

  • 300 g cream cheese (Philadelphia)
  • 300 g cold heavy cream
  • 100 g powdered sugar
  • 1½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ±300 g cookies
  • Whole milk (for dipping)
  • Sprinkles (for decoration)

OPTIONAL MODULE

  • Add a thin layer of (strawberry) jam between cookie layers.
  • Adjust cake size by changing the number of cookies per layer.

Process

  1. In a large bowl: combine cream cheese + powdered sugar + vanilla extract + heavy cream. Whip UNTIL stiff peaks form. Stop mixing when cream holds its shape.
  2. Select a serving dish or cake board.
  3. For each cookie layer (4 layers total): dip each cookie in milk (≈5 seconds), arrange cookies to form the base layer, add an even layer of cheesecake cream.
  4. Frost the cake: cover the top and sides completely with the remaining cream.
  5. Refrigerate: chill ≥ 4 hours (preferably overnight). Do NOT add sprinkles yet.
  6. Before serving: remove from the fridge 30 minutes in advance, decorate with sprinkles, slice and serve.

Result

A soft, layered no-bake cookie cake with a creamy texture and milk-softened cookie layers.

Storage

  • Keep covered in refrigerator.
  • Shelf life: 5–7 days.
