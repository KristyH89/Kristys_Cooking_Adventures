Easy Wrap Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 large tortilla wrap
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/4 onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Salami slices (optional)
  • Grated cheese
  • Fresh spring onion
  • Fresh basil leaves

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Mix ketchup + tomato paste + oregano + basil.
  3. Place wrap on baking tray. Spread sauce evenly.
  4. Top with bell pepper, onion, salami, cheese.
  5. Bake ~7 minutes UNTIL cheese melts and edges are crispy.
  6. Remove from oven. Finish with spring onion + fresh basil.

Result

A quick, crispy homemade wrap pizza with simple tomato sauce and your favorite toppings — perfect for a fast lunch or easy dinner.

Tip

Highly customizable — use any toppings you like: ham, chicken, mushrooms, olives, corn, spinach, pineapple, different cheeses, or extra vegetables.
