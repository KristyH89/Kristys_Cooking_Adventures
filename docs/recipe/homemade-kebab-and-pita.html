Homemade Moose Kebab (Crispy Oven-Baked Kebab Strips)

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced moose meat (or minced beef as an alternative)
  • 1 tbsp paprika powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)

OPTIONAL MODULES

  • Add 1 tbsp yogurt for softer texture.
  • Add 1 tbsp oil for richer flavor.
  • Use lamb mince for döner-style kebab.

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C.
  2. Place minced moose meat (or beef) in a bowl. Add all spices. Mix UNTIL fully combined and smooth.
  3. Shape mixture INTO a tight, compact loaf. Place loaf on baking tray.
  4. Bake 25 minutes at 175°C. Remove from oven. Let rest 10 minutes.
  5. Slice loaf INTO very thin strips. Increase oven temperature to 225°C.
  6. Spread strips on baking tray. Bake 5–10 minutes UNTIL crispy and browned.

Result

Crispy, flavorful homemade kebab strips — perfect for wraps, pita, bowls, or pizza.

Tip

Serve in pita or flatbread with: salad, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, garlic sauce, kebab sauce, or chili sauce.
